News

BTS’s unreleased ”Proof” is starting controversy within the music community. Some ARMY are boycotting the anthology album due to Jimin’s solo song “Filter” being written by Bobby Chung who has been accused of sexual assault. Song, Bobby’s ex girlfriend, commited suicide shortly after making the accusation. “Regarding this situation, quite a few fans are pointing out that it is not appropriate to include "Filter" on BTS' upcoming release, which will hit shelves on June 10. Since BTS has been relaying anti-violence and anti-discrimination messages for many years through its music, a lot of followers believe the septet should remove "Filter" so as to show its sincerity.” (TheKoreaTimes)
