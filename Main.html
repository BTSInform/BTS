Who is BTS?

Bangtan Sonyeondan, also known as BTS, is a South Korean boyband known for their global success in a wide range of areas, most notably their music. They hold many world records and are considered the most popular and successful band in the world at the moment. BTS speaks about a wide range of perplexing topics related to and including depression, anxiety, and self love.

Collaborations Outside of Music

Samsung is a current sponsor of BTS and has a few BTS inspired products. Other companies such as Hyundai, SIDIZ, Coway, Louis Vuitton, and Fila have also collabed with the band as well as so many other great companies. BTS has been featured on many popular magazine labels such as Time, Vogue, Dicon, GQ, and The Rolling Stone.

Current Records

BTS holds twenty-five records total in many subjects including social media, entertainment, cultural topics, and of course music. This has awarded them a spot in Guinness World Record's 2022 Hall of Fame.

Here are a few of their current records:

  • Most viewed YouTube music video in 24 hours ("Butter")
  • First K-pop act to reach No.1 on the US albums chart
  • Highest annual earnings for a K-Pop band
  • Most weeks at No.1 on Billboard’s Digital Song Sales chart
  • Most streamed act on Spotify

    Pre Debut


    Pre debut life wasn't very easy for the members, they suffered a lot of trouble during these years. Coming from a poor company they had to share one dorm room up until the year 2016. even having to choose between eating and a bus ride to the studio.

    Debut


    BTS debuted as a Hip Hop group with the album 2 Cool 4 Skool and their music quickly fell off the charts due to lack of exposure. Life was hard after their debut, the members were mistreated by multiple variety shows, usually performing as fill-ins with little screen play. Having to hand out flyers advertising free concerts, BTS really struggled with getting their music recognized and nearly broke apart, luckily they pulled through and were able to release "I Need U," their first hit song to kickstarted their sucess. Now having a bigger budget to produce greater music videos, BTS was able to climb to the top spot in the music industry, earning them a spot as the official South Korean presidential envoys.

    ARMY

    ARMY is BTS's fanbase and is considered to be the biggest and strongest fanbase in the world. We are a very diverse community consisting of people all over the world. We are men and women ranging from all ages, ethnicities, races, and religions who care deeply for the seven members with the same main interest of listening and appreciating BTS's music.
